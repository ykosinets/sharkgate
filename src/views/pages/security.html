Allowing IP addresses/ranges

This option allows you to whitelist trusted IP addresses (certain visitors, your website developer, etc) so that they will not be blocked by any of the SharkGate firewall protection. This basically gives them full unprotected access to your site so please only whitelist IP addresses that you are absolutely certain are safe visitors and have clean uninfected computers.

Separate IP addresses/ranges you want to whitelist with a comma (,) or new line (enter)

Blocking IP addresses/ranges

This option allows you to fully block access to your site from certain IP addresses. So block certain visitors you noticed are causing you issues. Or even block some competitor you would rather just keep away from copying or attacking your site. Once added that visitor will not be able to view or use any pages on your website.
